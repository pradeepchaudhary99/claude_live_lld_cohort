What You'll Learn

A 6-Week Structured Curriculum

Every session builds on the last. By week 6, you'll have solved 15+ real LLD problems and done mock interviews.

Week 1

Foundations & Thinking Framework

  • SOLID Principles — applied, not theoretical
  • OOP mastery: abstraction, encapsulation, polymorphism
  • 5-step LLD interview framework
  • How to do requirements clarification
Week 2

Design Patterns in Practice

  • Creational: Singleton, Factory, Builder, Prototype
  • Structural: Adapter, Decorator, Facade, Proxy, Composition
  • Behavioral: Observer, Strategy, Chain of Responsibility, state
  • Pattern selection under interview conditions
Week 3

Real LLD Problems Basic Level

  • Parking Lot, Elevator, Library System
  • Chess, Snake & Ladder, Tic-Tac-Toe
  • Splitwise, Food Delivery, Ride Sharing
  • Live coding with real-time review
Week 4

Advanced LLD Problems – Real World Systems (Part 1)

  • Design Amazon Locker System (slot allocation, delivery workflow, scalability considerations)
  • Design Movie Ticket Booking System (seat locking, concurrency handling, payment flow)
  • Design Hotel Booking System (availability search, pricing strategies, booking lifecycle)
  • Design Splitwise / Expense Sharing App (settlement strategies, balances)
  • Deep dive into Concurrency & Thread-Safety (race conditions, synchronization, locks)
  • Applying SOLID & Design Patterns in real-world LLD problems
Week 5

Advanced LLD Problems – Core Systems & Patterns (Part 2)

  • Design LRU Cache (HashMap + Doubly Linked List, O(1) operations)
  • Design Rate Limiter (Token Bucket, Leaky Bucket, Sliding Window)
  • Design Task Scheduler (priority-based execution, delayed jobs, retries)
  • Design Notification System (multi-channel: email, SMS, push)
  • Design Logging Framework (log levels, appenders, extensibility)
  • Design In-Memory File System (directories, files, operations)
  • Design Vending Machine (state machine, inventory, payment handling)
Week 6

Mock Interviews

FAQ

Common Questions

Who is this course for?

This course is for working professionals (SDE-1, SDE-2 and beyond), final-year students, and freshers who are preparing to crack LLD rounds at product-based companies. You should know basic OOP and be comfortable writing code in any language.

Do I need prior LLD or design pattern knowledge?

No. The course starts from first principles — SOLID, OOP, basic patterns — and builds up to complex problems. Beginners are welcome, but you should know how to write a class and basic OOP.

What programming language is used?

Sessions are taught in Java, but the concepts (design patterns, principles, frameworks) are completely language-independent. If you know Python, C++, or any OOP language, you'll follow along easily.

What if I miss a live session?

All sessions are recorded and uploaded within 24 hours. You get lifetime access to every recording, so missing a session won't affect your learning.

What platform are sessions on?

Live sessions run on Zoom. Recordings and resources are shared via a private community portal.

Are mock interviews included?

Yes — every enrolled student gets at dedicated 1-on-1 mock interviews with peers and pradeep will be monitoring those sessions and feedback will be provided.

We offer a full refund within 3 days of the batch starting if you feel the course isn't right for you. After that, we don't offer refunds as content is actively being delivered.

One salary increment from a better job offer often pays for this course 100x over. But more importantly: the confidence and structured approach you build here compounds across every technical interview you take in your career.
