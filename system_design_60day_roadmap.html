// FINAL WEEK MANTRA

  • You're the architect, not the order-taker — drive the design conversation
  • Every decision needs a "why" and a "what tradeoff am I accepting"
  • Show cost awareness: "This adds $X/month, here's a cheaper alternative"
  • Proactively mention failure scenarios before the interviewer asks
  • Think out loud — senior interviews reward reasoning, not just answers
